💊 Pain Management
  • Ibuprofen 600 mg PO q6h (preferred — anti-inflammatory)
  • Acetaminophen 650–1000 mg PO q4–6h PRN
  • Alternating APAP + ibuprofen for multimodal control
  • Ice perineum first 24 h; sitz bath after 24 h
  • Tucks pads / Dermoplast spray for perineal comfort
  • Afterpains: worse with multiparas & breastfeeding; educate & reassure

🍼 Feeding
  • Feed 8–12x/24 hrs (every 2–3 hrs)
  • Latch: wide open mouth, lips flanged, chin touching breast
  • Colostrum days 1–3 → transitional milk → mature milk by ~2 wks
  • Signs of adequate intake: 6+ wet diapers/day after day 4, weight gain
  • Hunger cues: rooting, hand to mouth, fussiness (cry = late cue)
Cradle hold Football hold Side-lying Cross-cradle
  • Every 3–4 hrs; 1–3 oz/feed in first weeks
  • 20 kcal/oz standard formula
  • Discard unused formula within 1 hr of feeding
  • No honey before age 1 (botulism risk)
  • Responsive/paced bottle feeding recommended

  • Physiologic jaundice: appears after 24 hrs, peaks day 3–5
